About Shivam Jewellery

Founded in 1992 by Mr. Rajesh Shivam, a third-generation jeweler, Shivam Jewellery has grown from a small family workshop to one of India's most respected jewelry brands. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create jewelry that celebrates life's precious moments while honoring our rich cultural heritage.

We source only the finest materials - ethically mined diamonds, premium gold, and authentic gemstones from trusted suppliers worldwide. Each piece undergoes rigorous quality checks at our state-of-the-art facilities in Mumbai and Jaipur.

Our team of 45+ master craftsmen, many of whom have been with us for decades, bring traditional techniques and contemporary innovation together. Their dedication to perfection is what sets Shivam Jewellery apart.
